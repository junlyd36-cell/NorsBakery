Our Story

For three generations, the Nors Bakery has been serving our community with the finest handcrafted breads and artisan cakes. What started as a small family dream in 1952 has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution.

We use only the finest ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of love in everything we bake. From our signature sourdough that rises for 24 hours to our decadent chocolate cakes, every item is made fresh daily.
